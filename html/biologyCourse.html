Joining A Batch

Our online registration form asks you to provide details about your syllabus, textbooks, and what your school teacher is currently teaching you in class. We use this information to find a batch that is doing similar stuff to what you are doing in class currently. This helps us conduct classes without confusion and in harmony. We highly reccomend that you attend a FREE DEMO CLASS to ensure that you are comfortable with the batch and the teaching style of your tutor. We ecourage you to READ MORE about our enrollment process.

Batch Sizes

A maximum of 6 students make up 1 batch. However, we try our best not to place more than 5 students in 1 batch. A minimum of 3 students are needed to form a batch. Even during a demo class, the batch sizes do not exceed 6.

Class Timings

One teacher manages a maximum of 8 batches at a time, 7 days a week. Each teacher has 6 batches from 5pm to 10pm on monday to saturday, and 2 weekend batches on saturday and sunday from 11am to 4.15pm. Each class from monday to saturday in the evening is 90 minutes long and 3 times a week, and on saturday and sunday in the morning is 2hrs 15 mins long and 2 times a week. This gives each student a total of 4hrs 30 mins of class time.
